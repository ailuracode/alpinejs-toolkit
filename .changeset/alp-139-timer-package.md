Add `@ailuracode/alpine-timer` with `$timer.create()`, `$timer.countdown()`, `$timer.countup()`, and `$timer.stopwatch()` presets backed by a drift-resistant timing engine. Includes pattern-based `format()` helpers (`mm:ss`, `hh:mm`, `hh:mm:ss`, and sub-second tokens) plus `formatPattern` timer options.
