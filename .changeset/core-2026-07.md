Remove `corePlugin()` and the `/head` subpath export. Drop the optional `@ailuracode/alpine-debug` peer dependency. Remove `defineMagicPlugin`, `defineStorePlugin`, `defineDirectivePlugin`, and `defineHybridPlugin` — `definePlugin(kinds, options)` is the only definition entry. Unify the registry's debug sink with `DebugLogger<TDetail>` from `core/debug`. Build migrated from `tsc` to `tsup`. Test runner now picks up `*.spec.ts` alongside `*.test.ts`.
